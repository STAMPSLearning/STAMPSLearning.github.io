Robotics Lessons


FLL, or First Lego League, is a STEM based robotics competition. The goal is to design a robot with LEGO parts and the EV3/NXT programmable brick.

Every year there is a new theme, and along with it a new playing field. The field contains 12-16 "mission models," or tasks, that the robot has to complete for points. Every registered FLL team will be sent a package at the beginning of every season with LEGO parts to build the "mission models" and the vinyl printed mat.
Sign up for FLL here.

There are three parts to FLL: a Robot Game, a Project part, and Core Values. Our videos and lessons will be focusing on the robot game aspect of FLL, but stay tuned for a series on the other parts. Your team will design a LEGO robot to try to complete as many tasks, or "mission models," on the field as possible in two and a half minutes. Points will be awarded for each task completed, respective to the difficulty of each mission.
