Lessons

Science:

Our science lessons walk you through a full scientific discovery. From analyzing a problem to designing a product, our intuitive videos show how you can plan out your own experiment that hasn't been done before. We here at STAMPS promote creation, and following directions for an experiment just isn't the same as designing one and seeing the results yourself.

Robotics:

A great program to get started with robotics and STEM is FLL. FLL is a LEGO robotics competition that includes a project part to it. Our videos and lessons give you complete guide to building and designing your own robot for the competition. The creators of STAMPS have 5 years of experience in FLL, and hope to share what they've learned to the teams, new or experienced.
