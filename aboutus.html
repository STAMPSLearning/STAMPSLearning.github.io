About Us

A team of high school students who want to share their passion with other young children

Our goal: STEM Inspiration


STAMPS aims to inspire young children about Science, Robotics, and Math problem solving. We plan to teach children through our online lessons, videos, seminars, workshops, summer camps and classes.



Introduction


STAMPS was founded by a team of high schoolers from the Bay Area in California. After 5 years of competing in the STEM based robotics competition FLL, members decided to present to the world the knowledge and passion they've picked up.

Lessons


We will have video lessons and articles to help teach children about STEM. We provide an intuitive way to learn science, robotics, and math problem solving. We will include breakdowns of a scientific discovery, an in-depth look at how you can design your own robot, and math problem solving videos.

Seminars/Workshops


STAMPS plans to holds free seminars at community places like libraries and schools to promote STEM. We will hold workshops and classes for hands on learning and tutoring. Our seminars and workshops will first start being held in the Bay Area.

Topics


The topics we teach here at STAMPS are science, robotics, and math problem solving, the key cornerstones of STEM. We are the first to educate the public on these three aspects through easy to understand yet effective lessons.
